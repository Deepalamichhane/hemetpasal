Our Journey

The Helmet Story

2018

Humble Beginnings

Founded by a team of passionate riders, HelmetNepal began with a mission: to make high-quality helmets accessible to every Nepali motorcyclist.

2020

First Store Opening

We opened our first physical store to help customers try, test, and choose the right helmet for their needs.

2022

Safety Recognitions

Our helmets received recognition for exceeding safety standards and gaining the trust of thousands of riders.

2025

Nationwide Expansion

Now shipping across Nepal with plans for international reach, HelmetNepal is becoming the trusted name in rider safety.

Our Philosophy

Safety With Purpose

At HelmetNepal, we believe that safety is every rider's right. Our mission is to deliver high-performance helmets that combine protection, comfort, and style—empowering Nepali riders to hit the road with confidence.

  • DOT & ECE certified safety
  • Advanced impact protection
  • Lightweight & aerodynamic designs
  • Accessible safety gear for all
